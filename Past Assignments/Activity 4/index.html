Color

Froges are mostly different shades of green so they can camofroge into their environment but sometimes they are pretty cool colors, to warn predators not to eat them because they are poisonous.

Eyes

Froges have big eyes that have a secondary eyelid that allows them to see underwater as if they had goggles. They also usually use the eyeballs to help them digest! They close their eyes and push the food down their bodies with them. Froges are so cool.
